Question 2: What is the difference between colspan and rowspan in tables? Provide examples.

ans:- 
    1. Colspan :-
        -> Expands a horizontally across multiple columns.
        -> <td colspan="2"> merges 2 columns into one.

    2. Rowspan :-
        -> Expands a Vertically across multiple rows.
        -> <td rowspan="2> merges 2 rows into one.
